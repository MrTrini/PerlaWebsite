NoMad Residences Wynwood

Wynwood's first hospitality-branded residences — now delivered, with unrestricted short-term-rental flexibility.

NoMad Residences Wynwood is the arts district's first hospitality-infused condominium — a NoMad-branded, Related Group project that is now completed and largely sold, one block from Wynwood Walls.

The opportunity

Designed by Arquitectonica with interiors by Design Agency, NoMad delivers 329 fully furnished, turnkey studio-to-two-bedroom residences (roughly 464–931 sq ft) built for rental flexibility — day, month, or year. It's an income model purpose-built for Wynwood's constant foot traffic.

The lifestyle is the product: a rooftop pool with the NoMad bar and restaurant, Casa Tua Cucina at ground level, the NoMad Library lounge, an outdoor cinema, and 24-hour concierge. Now delivered and 90%+ sold — proven demand, with limited remaining inventory.
