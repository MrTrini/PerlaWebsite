ELLE Residences Miami

The world's first ELLE-branded residences — fashion-inspired, turnkey living in rising Edgewater.

ELLE Residences Miami is the first-ever residential project branded by ELLE, the Parisian fashion magazine — a 25-story, 180-residence tower in Edgewater with décor inspired by the magazine and an attainable branded-residence entry price.

The opportunity

Developed by Vertical Developments and Urban Network Capital in partnership with ELLE, the tower offers turnkey one- and two-bedroom residences and penthouses (roughly 450–845 sq ft). The fashion-brand novelty gives it a distinct lifestyle identity in a neighborhood that's appreciating fast.

Amenities lean into a French Riviera sensibility: a resort sundeck with private cabanas, a rooftop pool, outdoor cinema, spa, high-tech gym, and a meditation garden. Groundbreaking is set for 2026 with completion around 2028 — an early entry point with a long appreciation runway.
