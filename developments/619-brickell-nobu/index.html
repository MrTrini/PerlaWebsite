619 Brickell by Nobu

Miami's first Nobu-branded residences — a Foster + Partners bayfront tower on Brickell Avenue.

619 Brickell brings Miami its first Nobu-branded residences: a Foster + Partners–designed bayfront tower on Brickell Avenue, rising roughly 75 stories directly on Biscayne Bay.

The opportunity

Developed by 13th Floor Investments with Key International, designed by Foster + Partners with Sieger-Suarez as architect of record, and with Nobu Hospitality operating dining and wellness. The tower's distinctive stacked, twisting form is oriented to maximize Biscayne Bay water views from every home.

The roughly 300 residences run from one-bedroom-plus-den to four-bedroom layouts, plus 19 sky villas and 4 penthouses, with 10-foot ceilings, Gaggenau kitchens and wraparound terraces. More than 90,000 square feet of amenities lean into wellness and longevity — a ground-floor Nobu restaurant (Miami's second), a Nobu wellness spa with cryotherapy and hyperbaric therapy, and four pools including an 86-foot sunset pool. Delivery is expected in 2030.

619 Brickell by Nobu FAQ

Where is 619 Brickell located?

At 619 Brickell Avenue, directly on Biscayne Bay in Miami's Brickell district.

What are prices at 619 Brickell?

Is there a Nobu restaurant on-site?

Yes — a ground-floor Nobu restaurant (Miami's second location), plus a residents-only Nobu café and in-residence Nobu dining.
