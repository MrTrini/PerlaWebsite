Downtown Miami Real Estate

The urban core — culture, waterfront, and the most connected address in the city, and one of the most accessible entries into luxury.

Downtown Miami is the city's urban core: cultural landmarks, bayfront parks, and unmatched transit connectivity, paired with some of the most accessible pricing in Miami's luxury-condo segment.

Why invest in Downtown Miami?

Downtown remained the most affordable entry point into Miami's luxury-condo market, closing Q1 2026 with a median around $730 per square foot; overall pricing spans roughly $605–$1,146 per square foot depending on the building's age and amenities. Momentum is strong — Greater Downtown recorded thousands of million-dollar transactions in early 2026, up more than 20% year over year.

Rental demand is deep and consistent, with average rents near $3,280 a month across unit types. For buyers who want an urban base with upside and liquidity, Downtown offers scale and a lower cost of entry than Brickell or the beaches.

What is it like to live in Downtown Miami?

Downtown is home to the Pérez Art Museum Miami, the Kaseya Center, Bayfront Park, and the Metromover — arguably the most connected part of the city, and one of the few where you can live largely car-free. It suits urban professionals, culture-minded buyers, and investors who value walkability and transit.

Questions, answered

Downtown Miami real estate FAQ

Is Downtown Miami a good value for luxury buyers?

Yes — Downtown has been the most affordable entry point into Miami's luxury-condo segment, closing Q1 2026 near $730 per square foot while offering strong culture, transit, and waterfront access.

What is the average condo price in Downtown Miami?

Prices generally run $605–$1,146 per square foot depending on the building, with a Q1 2026 luxury median around $730 per square foot.

Is Downtown Miami well connected?

It's the most transit-connected part of the city, with the free Metromover, Brightline and MetroRail access, and walkable proximity to Brickell, the arena, museums, and Bayfront Park.

What are rents like in Downtown Miami?

Average rents are around $3,280 per month, ranging from studios near $1,725 to high-end two-bedrooms above $7,600.
