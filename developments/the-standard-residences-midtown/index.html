The Standard Midtown

Miami's first true pied-a-terre — a completed, move-in-ready branded residence in the heart of walkable Midtown.

The Standard Residences, Midtown Miami brings The Standard's lifestyle brand to Miami's most pedestrian-friendly neighborhood: a completed, 12-story building of 228 move-in-ready pieds-a-terre, steps from the Design District and Wynwood.

The opportunity

Developed by Rosso Development and Midtown Development, the building offers 228 fully finished studio, one-bedroom, one-bedroom-plus-den, and two-bedroom residences from roughly 432 to 965 square feet. Because it's already delivered, buyers can close and move in — or begin renting — right away. Flexible rentals are permitted with a 30-day minimum, up to 12 times per year, making it a rare turnkey option among branded residences.

What it's like to live here

The Standard Midtown sits at 3100 NE 1st Avenue with more than 34,000 square feet of live/work/play amenities and 10,000 square feet of ground-floor retail. Midtown's walkable, round-the-clock energy is at the doorstep — Trader Joe's, Whole Foods, Sugarcane, and the shops of the Design District — with Wynwood and Downtown just minutes away.

The Standard Midtown FAQ

Is The Standard Residences Midtown completed?

Yes — it is delivered and move-in ready, so you can close and occupy or begin renting right away.

Can you rent out a unit at The Standard Midtown?

Yes — flexible rentals are allowed with a 30-day minimum, up to 12 times per year. Confirm current building rules before purchase.

What residence sizes are available?

Studios, one-bedrooms, one-bedroom-plus-dens, and two-bedrooms, from roughly 432 to 965 square feet, all fully finished.

Where is The Standard Residences Midtown located?

At 3100 NE 1st Avenue in Midtown Miami — steps from the Design District, Wynwood, and everyday retail like Trader Joe's and Whole Foods.
