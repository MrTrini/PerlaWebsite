Botanic Residences

A boutique West Brickell condo with no rental restrictions — fully furnished, design-forward living.

Botanic Residences is a boutique, design-forward condominium in West Brickell near the Miami River — just 51 residences over eight stories, a lower-density alternative to Brickell's high-rises.

The opportunity

Developed by Forth Development with MICL, with architecture by CLA+D and interiors by 2ID Interiors, and a nature-inspired palette of natural tones and greenery. The headline draw is a rare no-rental-restrictions policy: owners can lease daily, monthly, seasonally or long-term, so homes work as primary residences, vacation homes or income-producing investments.

One- to three-bedroom layouts, including a two-bedroom-plus-den, run from about 573 to 1,083 square feet, all delivered fully furnished and finished with gourmet kitchens, custom vanities, in-unit washer and dryer, and smart keyless entry. Amenities include a resort-style pool, fitness center, co-working space and library. The building topped off in early 2026 and is moving toward completion.

Botanic Residences FAQ

Can I rent my unit on Airbnb?

Yes — Botanic has no rental restrictions and is approved for short-term rentals, from daily to long-term leasing.

What are prices at Botanic Residences?

One-bedrooms start around $498,000, two-bedrooms around $661,000, and three-bedrooms around $878,000 (2025–2026 figures, subject to change). Ask me for current availability.

Do the residences come furnished?

Yes — all 51 homes are delivered fully furnished and finished, including gourmet kitchens and in-unit washer and dryer.

What unit types are available?

One- to three-bedroom layouts, including a two-bedroom-plus-den, ranging from about 573 to 1,083 square feet.

What is the building's status?
