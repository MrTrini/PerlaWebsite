Parkside Residences

West Brickell's pre-construction condo-hotel — flexible, rental-ready ownership at a ground-floor entry price.

Parkside Residences marks the U.S. debut of BI Group, one of the world's largest homebuilders, with a condo-hotel in fast-growing West Brickell designed for rental flexibility and early-stage upside.

The opportunity

Designed by Kobi Karp, Parkside is an 8-story, 187-unit building that blends 164 condo-hotel units with 21 private residences. Studios, one-, and two-bedroom layouts open to floor-to-ceiling glass. Because most units carry a hotel designation, owners get genuine short-term-rental flexibility — a rare feature in Brickell.

The project broke ground in April 2026 with delivery targeted for 2028, which puts buyers at the very start of the pre-construction appreciation window, backed by a developer with 150,000+ homes to its name.

Parkside Residences FAQ

Is Parkside Brickell short-term-rental friendly?

Yes — most units carry a hotel designation, giving owners short-term-rental flexibility. Confirm the current program terms before purchase.

Who is the developer of Parkside Residences?

BI Group, one of the world's largest homebuilders (150,000+ homes), with Parkside as its first U.S. project. Sales are handled by ONE Sotheby's International Realty.

When will Parkside Brickell be completed?

It broke ground in April 2026 with delivery targeted around 2028.

What are prices at Parkside Brickell?

Marketing figures start in the $550,000s as of 2026; some early materials cited lower entry points. Ask me for current pricing and availability.
