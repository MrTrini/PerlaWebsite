Domus Brickell Park

A completed, turnkey West Brickell condo-hotel built for short-term-rental income.

Domus Brickell Park is a completed, fully furnished West Brickell condo-hotel engineered for short-term-rental income — the first Brickell-area project to top off in 2025, now delivered with strong early sales velocity.

The opportunity

Developed by North Development (Edifica + Oak Capital) and designed by Zyscovich, Domus offers 171 fully furnished studios, one- and two-bedrooms (roughly 330–979 sq ft). Its proprietary rental program plugs into 85+ channels — Airbnb, Booking, Expedia, Marriott — making it a genuine turnkey income engine.

Amenities include a 4,200+ sq ft lobby, café and wine bar, artisanal market, rooftop pool deck with cabanas and a hot tub, gym, steam room, coworking, and bike share. Now delivered and de-risked, with 120 closings in its first 60 days.
