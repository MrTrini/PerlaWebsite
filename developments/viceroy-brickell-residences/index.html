Viceroy Brickell Residences

Five-star, Viceroy-branded living in the heart of Brickell's financial district.

Viceroy Brickell Residences brings the Viceroy luxury-hotel brand to the core of Brickell: a 45-story, 498-residence tower by Related Group with Meyer Davis interiors and full five-star hotel services.

The opportunity

Developed by Related Group with GTIS Partners and managed by Viceroy Hotels & Resorts, the tower offers fully finished studio-to-three-bedroom residences with hotel-grade service. The brand and location target affluent lifestyle buyers who want turnkey luxury in the financial district.

Amenities run deep: a resort pool and amenity deck, poolside café, spa and wellness center, screening room, wine-tasting salon, billiards lounge, and a racing simulator studio. The building is under construction — ask me to confirm the current delivery timeline.
