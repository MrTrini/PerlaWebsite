The Standard Residences, Brickell

A branded riverfront tower from The Standard — lifestyle-driven living in the heart of Brickell.

The Standard Residences, Brickell brings the globally recognized lifestyle brand to the Miami River: a 46-floor, 422-residence tower designed by Arquitectonica with one of the deepest amenity programs in the neighborhood.

The opportunity

Developed by Newgard and Two Roads, The Standard delivers fully finished studios to three-bedroom residences with nine-foot ceilings and Miele appliance packages. The brand pedigree carries real rental and resale cachet with a design-conscious, lifestyle-minded audience.

The amenity roster is the draw: a rooftop pool and club lounge with bay views, a 10th-floor wellness spa with hammam, sauna and cold plunge, a bowling alley, screening room, coworking lounge, and pet spa. Delivery is expected in 2027.
