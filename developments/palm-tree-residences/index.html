Palm Tree Residences Miami

The first branded residence from Palm Tree Crew — turnkey, rental-friendly living in Park West.

Palm Tree Residences is the first-ever branded residence from Palm Tree Crew — the global festival and hospitality brand founded by Kygo and Myles Shear — a 37-story, 483-home tower in Park West, Downtown Miami.

The opportunity

Developed by PMG with Lion Development, Sterling Equities and Eden Residential, and designed by Kobi Karp. Every residence is delivered fully furnished and turnkey, and the building is short-term-rental friendly — built with investor buyers in mind. Ownership doubles as membership into the Palm Tree Crew ecosystem, with priority festival access and members-only hospitality.

Layouts run from furnished junior suites through three-bedroom residences, priced from roughly $500,000 to $1.8M. The 57,000-plus square feet of amenities span three levels: a ground-floor Palm Tree Club venue, a rooftop beach club with resort-style pool, a double padel court, golf simulator, Technogym fitness center and a wellness spa. It sits steps from the Brightline station, Miami Worldcenter and Kaseya Center. Delivery is expected around 2030.
